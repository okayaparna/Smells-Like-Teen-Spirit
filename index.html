SMELLS LIKE TEEN SPIRIT
This project is a collection of objects commonly used by impulsive young adults in the energetic city of New York on a wild night out. The project offers a candid and honest look at the fun, and sometimes unpredictable, nature of nightlife culture.
Tonight you will get to experience New York's party scene through collections of objects that are commonly used by teenage party-goers. You will begin the night by chosing your fake Id at the door of the club, followed by chosing the music that you want to hear at the club. Now that you've danced a bit, you take a quick smoke break, where you meet a stranger and decide to spend the night safely with them.
